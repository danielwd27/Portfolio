Peer Mentor/TA for FYEX 101 - Hullabaloo U, Texas A&M

August 2024 - December 2024

In my Sophomore year, I had the pleasure of working as a TA for an introductory course called Hullabaloo U at A&M. I got to work with a great instructor who provided me with opportunities for independence and support when needed. As a peer mentor, I helped students navigate academic challenges by presenting lectures and leading activities alongside my instructor. We covered a wide range of topics covering ideas from study strategies to relationships and mental health. Furthermore, throughout the semester, I held one-on-ones with every student, getting to know them personally and hear about their goals. Although I haven't had the time to teach more sections of this class, I did really enjoy the semester.
